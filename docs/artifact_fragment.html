How to Scan a Slate
Routine game: DRatings and BPP agree on the winner, totals sit close to market, betting splits move together, starters are near their season norms, and there's no extreme weather/park effect. Nothing here needs a second look.

Notable game: one or more of the six checks above trips. Notable doesn't mean "bet this" — it means there's more disagreement between sources than usual, which is exactly the kind of case worth comparing against the actual final score afterward.
Personal learning project: compares model/market/public-sentiment data to actual outcomes over time. No betting units, stake sizing, or recommended plays are included or implied anywhere in this report.
