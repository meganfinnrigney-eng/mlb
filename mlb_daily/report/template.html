5. How to Scan a Slate

Routine game: DRatings and BPP agree on the winner and their totals are close together, the model total sits near the market total, betting splits (bets% vs money%) move together, and starting pitchers are pitching close to their season norms with no extreme weather/park effect. Nothing here needs a second look.

Notable game: one or more of the six checks below trips — the models disagree, the model and market total diverge, the betting public and the money are pulling in different directions, a starter is well outside their seasonal form in this game's context (home/road), the park/weather effect is unusually large, or MoundEdge's own trend arrow contradicts the numbers next to it. Notable doesn't mean "bet this" — it means there's more disagreement between sources than usual, which is exactly the kind of case worth comparing against the actual final score afterward.
